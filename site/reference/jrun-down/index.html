jrun down¶

Destroy jails and clean up their mounts, ports, and processes.

jrun down

Interactively select which jails to destroy.

Specific jails¶

Destroy named jails directly:

jrun down <name...>

jrun down hypha-python-314

Other jails are left untouched.
